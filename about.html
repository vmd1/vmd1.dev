About Me

My journey into tech started with a simple curiosity for how computers work, and it has since grown into a full-blown obsession. When I'm not coding, you can find me gaming, reading up on the latest tech trends, or participating in cybersecurity challenges. I'm a firm believer in lifelong learning and am always on the lookout for new skills to acquire and new challenges to overcome.

This website serves as my personal portfolio and blog, where I document my projects, share my learnings, and connect with like-minded individuals. Feel free to explore my work and reach out if you'd like to collaborate or just chat about tech.
